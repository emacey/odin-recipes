Boiling Water Recipe

Home

Description:

I have been wondering what all the fuss was about (you hear about it on all the cooking shows these days), but we tried this and the boiled water was FANTASTIC! A++! Tip: for a nice treat on a summer day, try it refrigerated. Pour over a glass full of ice... the kids love it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ice cube
  • 2 cups water

Steps:

  1. Place water in large enough pot to hold 3 cups without boiling over.

  2. Add ice cubes & Turn burner on high.

  3. Bring to a boil.

  4. Remove pot from burner.

  5. Turn burner off.

  6. *Use immediately or you will have to repeat recipe*

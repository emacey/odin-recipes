Krabby Patty Recipe

Home

Description:

The Krabby Patty is made out of a frozen patty with fresh lettuce, crisp onions and tomatoes with sea cheese, pickles, mustard, ketchup, and the KRABBY PATTY SECRET FORMULA.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Barnacle Shavings (For Calcium)
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Love (Cooking With Passion)
  • Chum
  • Seaweed Paste
  • [REDACTED]

Steps:

  1. Grill the patties until they are almost cooked through.

  2. Add the cheese on top and cook until melted.

  3. Spread the mustard and ketchup on the buns.

  4. Put a patty on top of each.

  5. Add the lettuce, tomatoes, pickles, onions and THE SECRET FORMULA.

  6. Top with the top bun and serve.
